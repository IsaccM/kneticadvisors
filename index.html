Who We Are

About KNETIC Advisors

A BC-based privacy advisory firm built specifically for the healthcare sector.

KNETIC Advisors Inc. was founded to fill a critical gap in the BC healthcare landscape: the need for dedicated, practical, and affordable privacy compliance support for regulated health practitioners and clinics.

As the Health Professions and Occupations Act (HPOA) comes into force on April 1, 2026, BC's regulated health professionals face new, more stringent privacy obligations. Many practices don't have the internal capacity — or the budget — to hire a full-time Privacy Officer. KNETIC was built to change that.

We combine deep knowledge of BC's healthcare regulatory environment with hands-on, on-site assessment methodology. We don't just deliver a report and walk away — we partner with your practice to implement real, sustainable privacy practices.

Our Mission

To make meaningful privacy compliance accessible and practical for every regulated health practice in British Columbia — regardless of size.

Our Values
  • Practical over theoretical — recommendations your team can actually implement
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • Patient-centred — privacy compliance ultimately protects the people your practice serves
  • BC-first — deep understanding of local regulatory colleges and HPOA requirements
  • Ongoing partnership, not one-time engagements
